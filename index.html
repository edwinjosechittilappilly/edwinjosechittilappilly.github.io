About Me

"Research is seeing what everybody else has seen and thinking what nobody else has thought."
— Albert Szent-Györgyi

Greetings! I'm Edwin Jose, an aspiring Technocrat and a passionate AI ML Enthusiast. Presently, I'm engaged as a Teaching Assistant at Western Michigan University, where I have the privilege of nurturing the minds of future technologists.

My journey in the world of technology led me to co-found Hello Ai Pvt Ltd, where I proudly serve as the Chief Technology Officer. At HAI Labs, our mission is to provide a dynamic web platform for young minds aged 9 to 15, enabling them to explore, learn, and apply the fascinating concepts of Machine Learning and Data Science. It's truly inspiring to witness the next generation embracing these cutting-edge technologies with such enthusiasm.

My early experiences in research laid a strong foundation for my career. I had the honor of working as a Research Fellow for the Sony Consultancy project at the Department of Electronics, CUSAT. Subsequently, I served as a Junior Research Fellow for the DST Project at the Department of Electronics, Cochin University of Science and Technology, delving into the depths of knowledge and innovation.

Education has always been a cornerstone of my journey. I earned my MSc in Electronics with a specialization in Machine Learning, securing the first rank at Cochin University of Science and Technology. Additionally, I'm an alumnus of Christ University, Bangalore, where I honed my skills and nurtured my passion.

My intellectual curiosity has led me to contribute to various research domains. I've authored several papers in fields ranging from Edge ML to face recognition and Time-series Analytics, reflecting my dedication to pushing the boundaries of knowledge.

Beyond academia, I've been involved as a technical advisor for the local internet portal irinjalakuda.com, showcasing my commitment to applying technology for the betterment of the community. I've also had the privilege of serving as the Kerala State Secretary of the Kites Foundation. During my tenure, I led the organization of Rang 1.0, one of the largest online youth festivals, leaving an indelible mark on the cultural landscape. Moreover, I played a pivotal role in creating and structuring the Kerala state and district committees, fostering growth and collaboration.

Presently, I hold the esteemed position of Ex Officio Member on the Kites Foundation Board of Directors. This role allows me to contribute to the organization's strategic direction and continue my commitment to positive change through technology and community engagement.

Join me on this incredible journey of exploration, innovation, and making a meaningful impact through technology. Let's shape the future together.
